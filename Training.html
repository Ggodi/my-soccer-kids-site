About Our Training

3 TO 5 YEARS OLD Soccer kid star age 3 to 4 training programme is designed for that age group. The goal is to introduce them to the basic concepts of soccer (dribbling, kicking, running, scoring goals), help them improve their social skills, and most importantly, have FUN. 

Kids will start getting familiar with soccer's structures, rules, and discipline through fun games and significant interaction, constantly changing activities that keep them focused. The priority is to offer kids an enjoyable experience and learning simultaneously. Every child enjoys training classes and develops skills in their unique ways.

6 TO 7 YEARS OLD The goal is to intrude these age groups to a soccer Drill, including Dribble through gates, kicking, scoring goals, Dribble to pass, pass through Gates and shooting. There is a lot of fun at this stage. 

Kids will truly enjoy game sessions even though there is a minimum of competition in this stage of learning; however, it is vital because education is associated with pleasant, fun, and social experiences that will provide a foundation for the future star. 

Once our kids-star get involved, they are happy to get ineffective soccer practice, including various soccer training activities.

YOUTH 7-12 YEARS OLD Soccer-Kid-Star youth 7 – 12 the goal is to help youth Star players to develop speed, build confidence, and maintain health, fitness, strength, and endurance. 

In addition, youth Star has plenty of fluids to rehydrate; parents may not worry too much if they forget fluids. At this stage, we are dedicating significant efforts to developing competitive skills through various training activities. 

The youth soccer teams provide a structured environment for kids to learn the fundamental of the sport. This is when our coaching team works to improve youth passing skills, dribbling Drill, and improving kicking the ball.

What We do

We are training kids to become a soccer football stars. Our coaching style is always planned and supervised training sessions. Our teamwork focuses on specific areas, such as defensive or offensive play, goalkeeping, and soccer drill. 

In addition, Training also involves free kicks, dribbling and corner kicks. A significant part of a child's soccer experience is coaching. Our Coaches are energetic, enthusiastic, the most qualified, and constantly learning to adapt to change. The entire Soccer kid stars consider coaching as the most crucial part of a child's soccer development. This is because coaches play a vital role in a child's behaviour—that is why our coaches work very closely with parents or teachers.
